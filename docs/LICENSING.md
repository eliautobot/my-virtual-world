# Licensing

My Virtual World is **source-available**, not OSI-approved open source. Its
default licenses allow noncommercial inspection, use, modification, and
sharing while reserving commercial rights.

## What Each License Covers

| Material | Default terms |
| --- | --- |
| Software source code and related technical documentation | [PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0](../LICENSE) |
| Project-owned artwork, screenshots, maps, world layouts, models, textures, audio, and similar creative assets | [CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](../ASSETS-LICENSE.md) |
| My Virtual World name, logos, wordmarks, and other brand identifiers | All rights reserved; see [TRADEMARKS.md](../TRADEMARKS.md) |
| Third-party components or material | Their respective licenses and notices |

If a specific file includes a different license or attribution notice, that
specific notice controls for that file.

## Noncommercial Use

The PolyForm Noncommercial license permits noncommercial use, modification,
and distribution of the software, subject to its full terms and notice
requirements. The creative-assets license similarly permits noncommercial
sharing and adaptation with attribution and share-alike requirements.

These licenses do **not** permit resale, paid hosting, bundling into a paid
product, commercial consulting deployments, internal business use, or other
commercial exploitation unless a separate written license grants those
rights. PolyForm Noncommercial restricts commercial use broadly, not only the
resale of copies.

## Commercial Licensing

A separate commercial license is required for any commercial use. Visit
[myvirtualworld.ai](https://myvirtualworld.ai/) for commercial licensing and
permission requests.

An application License Key unlocks product features. It does not change the
repository's source or asset licenses unless the applicable purchase terms or
a separate written agreement expressly grant additional rights.

## Redistribution Requirements

When sharing a permitted noncommercial copy or fork:

1. Include the PolyForm license terms or their official URL.
2. Preserve every line that begins with `Required Notice:`.
3. Preserve applicable creative-asset attribution and CC BY-NC-SA terms.
4. Do not use My Virtual World branding in a way that implies the fork is the
   official product or is endorsed by My Virtual World.
